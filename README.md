# FraudShield AI

## Overview

FraudShield AI is an AI-powered digital fraud detection and citizen protection platform designed to identify scam messages, digital arrest frauds, counterfeit currency threats, and suspicious financial activities.

## Problem Statement

India faces a growing number of cyber frauds, digital arrest scams, phishing attacks, and financial fraud cases. Citizens often struggle to identify fraudulent communications before becoming victims.

## Solution

FraudShield AI analyzes suspicious messages and communications, generates a fraud risk score, and provides actionable recommendations to help users avoid scams.
